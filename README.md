# YouTube Subscribed Posts Viewer

A lightweight browser extension that adds a `Subscribed Posts` button to YouTube and shows recent community posts from the channels you subscribe to.

It runs directly on `youtube.com` and fetches subscribed channels and their posts from YouTube's internal endpoints.

## Features

- Adds a `Subscribed Posts` button to the YouTube left sidebar
- Collects recent community posts from subscribed channels
- Shows posts in a compact modal with channel icon, name, relative time, and text
- Opens each post in a new tab
- Refreshes cached entries in the background when possible
- Limits stored posts to the most recent 100 entries

## Usage

1. Open [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/).
2. In the left navigation, click `Subscribed Posts`.
3. Wait while the extension loads your subscribed channels and recent posts.
4. Click any post card to open the original post on YouTube.
